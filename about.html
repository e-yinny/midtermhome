About Us

Best Catering Company
East of the Mississippi

Chef Danimal Olive visited Boston, Massachusetts in the early 2000's to find a place to fulfill a longtime culinary dream. As a child, he was fascinated with the movie Ratatouille, and wanted the entire world to be able to enjoy food like the characters in the movie. He started perfecting the dish at age 15, and after he graduated from culinary school he decided to start Ratatouille Catering. Our catering company prides itself in providing the best Ratatouille east of the Mississippi, leaving everyone satisfied everytime.
